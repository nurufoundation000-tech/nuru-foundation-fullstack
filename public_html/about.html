About Nuru Foundation: Empowering Individuals in Kenya with Coding & AI Skills

Nuru Foundations is commited to empowering youth through innovative education and technology, fostering creativity, leadership, and practical skills that shape a brighter, knowledge-driven future.

Explore Our Journey

From a Spark to a Brighter Future!

NURU FOUNDATION began with a simple yet powerful vision, to make quality learning accessible to everyone, especially the young and energetic minds within our communities. We recognized a growing need for engaging, modern education that goes beyond traditional textbooks - and we decided to light that path.

We are a youth-driven initiative, powered by the very people we aim to empower! Our mission is to create a learning environment where curiosity thrives, creativity blossoms, and education becomes an exciting adventure. From the brilliance of Mathematics to the innovation of AI Essentials, we help learners discover, explore, and grow.

Our Guiding Stars

Our Mission

To illuminate minds through technology, innovation, and real knowledge - empowering young people to build, think, and create solutions that shape a better tomorrow.

Our Vision

To become Africa's leading youth-driven foundation in transforming education through creativity, technology, and human development - inspiring a generation that learns to build, not just consume.

Lighting the Spark of Smart Learning!"

Holistic Development

We don't just teach; we nurture the whole person! Our programs blend core academics like Mathematics and Physics with crucial Soft Skills, Ethics, Financial Literacy, and Career Guidance.

Future-Ready Skills

Dive into Computer Literacy, Programming, Databases, and AI Essentials empowering learners with practical, future-ready skills for the jobs of tomorrow.

Community-Centric

We connect with students, parents, schools, churches, and community groups. Learning is a shared journey, and we keep everyone informed and involved every step of the way.

Engaging Virtual Platform

Our virtual classroom is interactive, dynamic, and engaging - designed to make learning both effective and enjoyable. With recorded sessions and LMS, we're reimagining what online education can be.

Your 12-Month Mathematics Journey

📘 PHASE 1: FOUNDATIONS (Months 1–4)

  • Month 1: Number Theory & Arithmetic – whole numbers, LCM & HCF, ratios, and word problems.
  • Month 2: Algebra Basics – variables, equations, sequences, and real-life problem solving.
  • Month 3: Geometry & Measurement – shapes, area, volume, coordinate geometry, and symmetry.
  • Month 4: Data & Probability – data collection, graphs, mean/median/mode, basic probability.

📗 PHASE 2: INTERMEDIATE LEVEL (Months 5–8)

  • Month 5: Intermediate Algebra – quadratic & simultaneous equations, inequalities, and functions.
  • Month 6: Trigonometry & Vectors – trig ratios, radians, vectors in 2D & 3D, real-life applications.
  • Month 7: Calculus Introduction – limits, differentiation, slopes, and real-life motion applications.
  • Month 8: Advanced Statistics & Probability – standard deviation, distributions, and data projects.

📙 PHASE 3: ADVANCED & APPLIED MATH (Months 9–12)

  • Month 9: Applied Math I – modeling, matrices, finance math, and geometric progressions.
  • Month 10: Applied Math II – optimization, linear programming, and motion modeling.
  • Month 11: Logic, Sets & Proofs – set theory, logic gates, reasoning, and math competitions.
  • Month 12: Capstone Project – integrate all skills into a real-world math project and presentation.

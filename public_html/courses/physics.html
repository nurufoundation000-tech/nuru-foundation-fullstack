Your 6–8 Month Physics Journey

📘 PHASE 1: FOUNDATIONS OF PHYSICS (Months 1–2)

  • Introduction to Physics: Meaning, importance, and key contributors (Galileo, Newton, Einstein).
  • Branches of Physics: Mechanics, Thermodynamics, Optics, Electricity, and Modern Physics.
  • Scientific Methods: Experimentation, observation, and problem-solving.
  • Measurement and Units: Physical quantities, SI units, precision, and instruments (vernier calipers, micrometer).
  • Motion and Kinematics: Speed, velocity, acceleration, and basic projectile motion.

📗 PHASE 2: MECHANICS AND FORCES (Months 3–4)

  • Newton’s Laws: Force, inertia, momentum, friction, and circular motion.
  • Work, Energy, and Power: Definitions, conservation of energy, and mechanical power.
  • Simple Machines: Levers, pulleys, and inclined planes — mechanical advantage and real-life applications.

📙 PHASE 3: HEAT, LIGHT, AND SOUND (Months 5–6)

  • Thermal Physics: Heat, temperature, expansion, and heat transfer methods.
  • Light (Optics): Reflection, refraction, lenses, mirrors, and optical instruments.
  • Sound: Nature of sound waves, frequency, amplitude, echo, and applications.

📕 PHASE 4: ELECTRICITY AND MODERN PHYSICS (Months 7–8)

  • Electricity Basics: Static charges, current, voltage, resistance, Ohm’s law, and circuits.
  • Magnetism & Electromagnetism: Magnetic fields, induction, motors, and generators.
  • Modern Physics: Atomic structure, radioactivity basics, and the future of nuclear & solar energy.
